What is UNAI??

Universitas Advent Indonesia (UNAI) is a higher education institution managed by the Seventh-day Adventist Church of the western union of Indonesia. The history of UNAI is that founded in Cimindi in 1929 which was originally called Opleiding School der Advent Zending. In the past, this school was founded for the education of the evangelists of the Seventh-day Adventist Church


Universitas Advent Indonesia has 6 faculties

1). Faculty of philosophy
2). Faculty of teacher training & education
3). Faculty of economics
4). Faculty of nursing
5). Faculty of mathematics & natural science
6). Faculty of information technology
